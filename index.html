Research Work

Research Papers

Legal Document Translation using Fine-tuned LLMs

Under Model Testing near future

Built a parallel corpus (≈10k sentence pairs) from Nepali and English legal texts using OCR (PyTesseract) and manual alignment/verification performed with law-student collaborators.Fine-tuned MBART50 and custom Transformer variants for domain adaptation; baseline BLEU around 0.42 (ongoing improvements focused on formatting, legal phrase preservation, and domain-specific tokenization)

NLP Legal Tech Machine Translation

Detecting Image Forgeries & Deepfakes: Comparative Study

NCCI 2025

Assembled a mixed dataset (manipulated + AI-generated) totaling over 140k images across multiple manipulation types for benchmarking image-forensics approaches. Implemented and benchmarked convolutional CNNs (InceptionV3 family) vs Vision Transformers; re- ported improved accuracy and efficiency for selected CNN baselines in our experiments (InceptionV3: 94% on our test split)

Computer Vision Machine Learning AI

Enhancing Ethical Reasoning in Tiny LLMs

NCCI 2025

Created synthetic ethical-dilemma datasets (≈1k cases) covering utilitarian, deontological, and virtue ethics perspectives for probing small LLMs. Fine-tuned TinyLlama-1.1B using LoRA-based methods to create specialized agents and evaluated consistency and robustness via human & automated metrics.

Ethical Reasoning NLP LLMs
